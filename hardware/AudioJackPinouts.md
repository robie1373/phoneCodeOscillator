#Audio jack pinouts

## TRRS Pinout

Sleeve | Ring 2 | Ring 1 | Tip
-------|--------|--------|----
Mic    | Ground | Right  | left

Where "Tip" is the actual tip of the plug and each section is separated by a plastic insulator.

## TRS Pinout

Sleeve | Ring  | Tip
-------|-------|-----
Ground | Right | Left
